About Us

Goodville University is a private liberal arts college located in the heart of a vibrant city. With a history dating back over 150 years, we are committed to providing our students with an exceptional education that prepares them for success in their chosen careers and in life.

Our academic programs are designed to challenge and inspire our students, and our dedicated faculty and staff are committed to providing a supportive and inclusive learning environment.

At Goodville University, we believe that a well-rounded education includes not only academic coursework, but also opportunities for personal and professional growth through extracurricular activities, community service, and internships.

Admissions

Thank you for your interest in Goodville University. We believe that the admissions process is an opportunity for us to get to know you and for you to learn more about what Goodville has to offer.

Our admissions team is here to help guide you through the application process and answer any questions you may have. We welcome applications from students of all backgrounds and experiences.

Application Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Official transcripts from all secondary schools attended
  • Standardized test scores (SAT or ACT)
  • One letter of recommendation
  • Personal statement
  • Bank Statements

Student Life

At Goodville University, we believe that learning extends beyond the classroom. Our student life programs provide opportunities for students to explore their interests, make connections, and engage with the campus community.

Clubs and Organizations

We have a wide variety of clubs and organizations on campus, ranging from academic groups to cultural clubs. Getting involved in a club is a great way to meet new people and explore your interests outside of class.

Athletics

We offer a range of intercollegiate and intramural sports programs for students of all levels. Our athletes compete in the NCAA Division III and are known for their hard work and dedication.

Community Service

At Goodville, we believe in the importance of giving back to our community. We offer a variety of community service programs and opportunities for students to get involved and make a difference.

Alumni

We are proud of our alumni and the contributions they make to their communities and the world. As a Goodville University graduate, you are part of a lifelong community that provides opportunities for networking, professional development, and personal growth.

Get Involved

  • Attend alumni events
  • Mentor current students
  • Make a donation to support scholarships and programs
  • Join an alumni affinity group
  • Update your contact information to receive the latest news and updates
